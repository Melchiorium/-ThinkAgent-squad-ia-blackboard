## Project Brief

Project Name: LocalLoop

Pitch:
A mobile application that helps people discover local independent businesses through personalized recommendations, neighborhood deals, and loyalty rewards.

Context:
Many consumers want to support local businesses but often default to large platforms because discovering small local stores, restaurants, services, and events is fragmented and inconvenient.

At the same time, independent businesses struggle with visibility and customer retention but often lack the budget or expertise to run digital marketing campaigns.

The platform helps consumers discover relevant local businesses while giving small businesses simple tools to attract and retain nearby customers.

Target Users:
- Urban residents
- Young professionals
- Families
- Tourists
- Independent local businesses

Potential Use Cases:
- Discover nearby coffee shops
- Find local restaurants with promotions
- Discover niche stores
- Access loyalty rewards
- Local event discovery
- Neighborhood recommendations

Platform Capabilities:
- Personalized recommendations
- Geolocation
- Merchant profiles
- Promotions/offers
- Loyalty system
- Reviews
- Local event feed

Constraints:
- Limited initial budget
- Need enough local business supply in one city before expanding
- Must avoid competing directly with massive horizontal platforms

Challenges:
- Convincing local businesses to join
- Maintaining recommendation quality
- Avoiding platform saturation with irrelevant offers

Long-term Vision:
Become the default discovery platform for local commerce and neighborhood experiences.

# LocalLoop MVP Product Proposal

## Product Problem
Consumers struggle to discover local independent businesses amidst the dominance of large platforms. Simultaneously, small businesses lack visibility and affordable tools to engage with nearby consumers effectively.

## Initial Wedge
Focus on urban residents seeking nearby coffee shops and restaurants with promotional offers while initially engaging a limited set of local independent businesses.

## First Target User
Urban residents, particularly young professionals and families looking for local dining and shopping options.

## Existing Alternatives And Switching Trigger
- Alternatives: Yelp, Google Maps, and large food delivery platforms that offer broad visibility but lack personalization for local businesses.
- Switching Trigger: LocalLoop must provide a more tailored discovery experience focused on personalization and loyalty rewards that these alternatives do not emphasize.

## Core MVP Workflow
1. Users install the LocalLoop app.
2. Users input location and preferences.
3. The app recommends a list of local independent businesses with current promotions.
4. Users can view merchant profiles, including reviews and loyalty offer details.
5. Users engage with selected businesses and redeem promotions.

## In Scope
- Personalized recommendations based on user location and preferences
- Geolocation feature to detect nearby businesses
- Basic merchant profiles with essential business information and current promotions
- Simple loyalty system for users who make purchases
- User reviews feature for feedback on businesses

## Out of Scope
- Evaluation of events or community activities beyond initial business offerings
- Detailed analytics tools for businesses (e.g., advanced marketing features)
- Nationwide expansion beyond the initial city

LocalLoop will monetize through a subscription model for businesses and potentially a small transaction fee on rewards redeemed by users.
