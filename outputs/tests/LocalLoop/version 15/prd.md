# LocalLoop MVP Proposal

## Product Problem
Consumers struggle to find and engage with local independent businesses, often defaulting to larger platforms. Independent businesses also lack visibility and efficient marketing tools to connect with their community, impacting customer retention and engagement.

## Initial Wedge
Enable urban residents to discover local independent coffee shops and restaurants through personalized recommendations and promotions, helping support local economies while providing easily accessible deals.

## First Target User
Urban residents aged 25-40, particularly young professionals who are interested in supporting local businesses and seeking convenience in discovering neighborhood options.

## Existing Alternatives And Switching Trigger
- **Alternatives**: Google Maps, Yelp, Facebook local groups, TripAdvisor.
- **Switching Trigger**: A seamless user experience that provides better, personalized local business recommendations and exclusive neighborhood deals unavailable on broader platforms.

## Core MVP Workflow
1. User signs up and provides preferences (type of coffee shop/restaurant, interest areas).
2. User views personalized home screen featuring local independent coffee shops and restaurants.
3. Users can view promotions available from selected businesses.
4. Users can navigate to the businesses and access loyalty rewards.

## In Scope
- User registration and profile setup.
- A simple onboarding path focused on minimal necessary preferences for engagement.
- Personalized recommendations engine for coffee shops and restaurants based on user preferences.
- Geolocation to show nearby businesses.
- Basic merchant profiles outlining relevant offers.
- Loyalty system to track user rewards.
- Access to user-generated reviews for trust-building.

## Out of Scope
- Events feed and promotions from non-coffee shop/restaurant businesses.
- Detailed analytics for businesses.
- Advanced features like social sharing or community forums.
- Integration with third-party payment systems.
- Full-scale loyalty track management.

## MVP Build Vs Pilot Operations
### Must Build Now
- User registration and profile setup.
- Simple onboarding focused on essential preferences.
- Personalized recommendations engine.
- Geolocation functionality.
- Basic merchant profiles.
- Loyalty rewards tracking.

### Manual Or Operational During Pilot
- Outreach to local businesses for early partnerships.
- Manual tracking of loyalty rewards before full integration.

### Deferred Until After Proof
- Expansion of categories beyond coffee shops and restaurants.
- Full-fledged event discovery features.
- Advanced analytics for business users.

## Business Model Hypothesis
Generate revenue through partnerships with local businesses who pay for promotional listings and potentially a commission on loyalty rewards redeemed.

## Critical Assumptions
- There is sufficient interest from local businesses to participate.
- Users will engage with the app to discover new local businesses.
- The recommendation algorithm will provide relevant options to users.

## How To Test Quickly
- Conduct surveys with local residents to validate interest.
- Pilot with a small group of selected local businesses to gather feedback on app functionalities.
- Measure user engagement through app analytics.

## Acceptance Criteria
- At least 100 local independent coffee shops and restaurants signed up for the pilot.
- 75% of pilot users report discovering at least one new business.
- User retention rate exceeds 60% after the first month.

## Risks And Failure Modes
- Difficulty in convincing local businesses to join the platform.
- The recommendation engine may not accurately classify user preferences.
- Users may find offers irrelevant, leading to low engagement.

## Product Readiness
Status: LIMITED

Blocking Gaps:
- Uncertainty around local business participation and user acquisition. [business_engagement]
- Concerns about the effectiveness of the recommendation algorithm. [algorithm_efficacy]

Required Improvements:
- Develop partnership strategies for local business onboarding. [partner_acquisition]
- Improve user experience for interest profiling. [user_experience]

## Recommendation
Proceed with the MVP focused on coffee shops and restaurants, validating assumptions through a focused pilot with a manageable number of local businesses. Adjust the business model as necessary based on initial user engagement and feedback.
