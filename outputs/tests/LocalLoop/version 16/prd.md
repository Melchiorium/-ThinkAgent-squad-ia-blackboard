# LocalLoop MVP Proposal

## Product Problem
Urban residents struggle to discover local independent businesses due to fragmented information. Independent businesses lack visibility and tools to retain customers, leading to lower foot traffic and sales.

## Initial Wedge
Provide personalized recommendations of nearby independent businesses to urban residents, focusing on partnership with local businesses to offer loyalty rewards directly linked to engagement metrics.

## First Target User
Urban residents aged 25-40, particularly young professionals who are motivated to support local businesses.

## Existing Alternatives And Switching Trigger
Current alternatives include large discovery platforms (e.g., Yelp, Google Maps). The switching trigger is a desire for personalized, relevant recommendations that support local economies, along with better loyalty rewards directly from businesses.

## Core MVP Workflow
1. User downloads and registers on LocalLoop.
2. User completes a brief preferences questionnaire.
3. User accesses a personalized feed of recommended local businesses based on location and preferences.
4. User can view merchants’ promotions and loyalty rewards, and choose to visit businesses.
5. User visits the business and redeems offers through the app.

## In Scope
- Personalized recommendations based on user preferences and location.
- Merchant profiles with basic information and promotions.
- Loyalty rewards feature enabling users to collect points for visits.
- User registration and profile setup.
- Clear onboarding process for local businesses to participate and understand value propositions.

## Out of Scope
- Advanced features like event discovery and user reviews for the MVP.
- Extensive marketing campaigns for businesses.
- In-app purchases beyond loyalty rewards.
- User-generated content like reviews.

## MVP Build Vs Pilot Operations
### Must Build Now
- User registration and profile setup.
- Personalized recommendations engine.
- Basic merchant profiles with promotions.
- Loyalty rewards tracking.

### Manual Or Operational During Pilot
- Outreach to local businesses for participation.
- Manual verification of business eligibility for promotions.
- User engagement tracking for evaluating interest in the app.

### Deferred Until After Proof
- Expanded features such as event discovery and user reviews.
- Marketing tools for merchants beyond basic profiles.
- The tiered rewards system to encourage higher engagement levels.

## Business Model Hypothesis
LocalLoop will monetize through partnerships with local businesses by offering promotional packages and a small percentage of loyalty rewards redeemed.

## Critical Assumptions
- Urban residents are motivated to use an app dedicated to discovering local businesses.
- Local businesses will see value in participating and offering promotions.
- The recommendations will meet user needs enough to drive engagement.
- Users will engage with the app, with at least 30% active within the first week of use.
- Incentives are sufficient for local businesses to participate and promote offers.

## How To Test Quickly
- Conduct user interviews and surveys to validate interest in preferred features.
- Launch a basic pilot in one urban area, testing the onboarding process and engagement with recommendations.

## Acceptance Criteria
- At least 100 local business profiles created with active promotions.
- A minimum of 500 registered user profiles within the first month of launch.
- At least 40% of users redeem a loyalty reward in the first three months.
- Validation that at least 30% of users engage with the app within the first week.

## Risks And Failure Modes
- Resistance from local businesses to join the platform.
- Low user engagement with listings and lack of visits to businesses.
- Poor recommendation quality that drives users away from the app.

## Product Readiness
Status: LIMITED

Blocking Gaps:
- User interest validation in personalized recommendations. [demand_validation]
- Business participation and the value of loyalty rewards for merchants. [business_participation]

Required Improvements:
- Refine user engagement strategies during the pilot phase. [engagement_strategy]
- Establish firm partnerships with a critical mass of local businesses. [partnerships]

## Recommendation
Proceed with the MVP focused on personalized recommendations and basic merchant profiles, ensuring a clear onboarding process for businesses. Pilot in a single urban area to validate user interest and engagement. Expand features only after confirming user and business participation.
