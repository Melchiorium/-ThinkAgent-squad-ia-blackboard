# Melody Product Proposal

## Product Problem
Current dating and social platforms often overlook the importance of shared musical interests, making it difficult for users to find deeper lifestyle compatibility. Fragmented music communities increase the challenge of connecting with like-minded individuals and discovering new music in a cohesive environment.

## Initial Wedge
The initial wedge for Melody is to connect singles based on music compatibility, leveraging shared tastes to create meaningful relationship opportunities. This feature directly addresses the gap in existing platforms where music is not considered a fundamental factor in matching.

## First Target User
The first target user segment will be singles who are passionate about music and seeking deeper connections through shared interests in music genres and artists.

## Existing Alternatives And Switching Trigger
Existing alternatives include conventional dating apps (e.g., Tinder, Bumble) and niche music platforms (e.g., Bandmix, Meetup). Users may switch to Melody if they feel limited by the broad, superficial matching systems of dating apps and seek a platform that integrates music into relationship building and community engagement.

## Core MVP Workflow
1. User onboarding to collect music preferences focused on specific genres and artists.
2. Compatibility scoring based on shared musical tastes.
3. Matching singles based on scores with messaging capabilities.
4. Recommendations for concerts and music events based on user profiles.

## In Scope
- Music taste onboarding focusing on genre and artist preferences.
- Compatibility scoring between matches solely based on user preferences.
- Messaging between matched users.
- Event discovery for concerts relevant to user interests based on onboarding data.

## Out of Scope
- Buying/selling music equipment.
- Extensive community group features.
- Searches for band members or musicians for jamming.
- Music marketplace transactions.
- Advanced artist recommendations beyond initial onboarding.

## Business Model Hypothesis
The primary revenue stream will come from premium features offering enhanced matching options and messaging capabilities. Additional revenue might be explored through partnerships with concert organizers and local venues. Validation of the business model's sustainability will be performed through user interest in premium features during initial user testing.

## Critical Assumptions
- Users will prioritize music compatibility as a key factor in dating.
- A significant market exists for singles seeking deeper connections through music.
- Users are willing to provide music preference data for improved matching.
- Securing partnerships with music platforms for data access is feasible.

## How To Test Quickly
- Conduct surveys and interviews with potential users to validate interest in music-based matchmaking.
- Develop a simple landing page that outlines the app's concepts to gauge initial interest through sign-ups.
- Identify potential music API providers and assess data integration and partnership opportunities.

## Acceptance Criteria
- At least 100 users complete the onboarding process.
- 70% of users report satisfaction with the compatibility scoring process.
- At least 30% of matches result in messaging interactions within the first week.
- Secured agreement with at least one music data API provider before launch.

## Risks And Failure Modes
- Low user density may lead to reduced matching opportunities.
- Users may not appreciate the music compatibility feature as valuable.
- The platform's dual functionality may dilute focus, deterring users primarily seeking dating options.
- Uncertainty regarding user satisfaction metrics related to matching and compatibility.

## Product Readiness
Status: LIMITED

Blocking Gaps:
- Insufficient data on user preference and behavior concerning music compatibility.
- Need to establish partnerships with music platforms for data access.

Required Improvements:
- More research on user motivations for prioritizing music in relationships.
- Validation of the proposed business model's sustainability.
- Establish definitive agreements with music APIs for necessary data access.

## Recommendation
Proceed with a limited MVP focused on music compatibility for singles, emphasizing user onboarding, compatibility scoring, and messaging. Validate the app's concept through user interviews and a landing page to test interest prior to development. Collaborate with relevant music API providers to secure data access for effective onboarding and scoring.
