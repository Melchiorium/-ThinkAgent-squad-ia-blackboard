# SkillBridge MVP Product Proposal

## Product Problem
Young graduates and career switchers face challenges in securing roles due to a lack of practical experience. Small companies, startups, and non-profits require assistance with short-term projects but typically lack the resources to hire full-time employees or pay for expensive agencies. SkillBridge aims to bridge this gap by facilitating connections between these two groups, creating an avenue for graduates to gain hands-on experience while helping organizations with pressing needs.

## Initial Wedge
Focus on matching recent graduates and career changers with small businesses and startups for short-term projects that can be completed remotely or on-site.

## First Target User
Recent graduates seeking practical experience and exposure to real-world projects in their respective fields.

## Existing Alternatives and Switching Trigger
Current alternatives include unpaid internships and platforms like Fiverr and Upwork. SkillBridge offers structured learning opportunities, clear project parameters, and a focused platform that emphasizes quality experiences, encouraging candidates to switch for potential future employment.

## Core MVP Workflow
1. Graduates create profiles detailing their skills and interests.
2. Companies post short-term projects outlining requirements and expectations, which undergo manual review for quality assurance.
3. The platform matches graduates with projects based on skill alignment.
4. Communication tools facilitate discussions and project details between graduates and companies.
5. Graduates complete the project, submit deliverables, and receive feedback from companies.

## In Scope
- User profiles for graduates and organizations, including essential data fields for accurate matching.
- Project posting capability for organizations with a manual review process to ensure quality.
- Basic matching system based on keyword alignment of skills and project requirements.
- Messaging system for candidate-organization communication.
- Deliverable submission functionality.
- Feedback and reputation system to foster trust between participants.
- Development of foundational legal compliance criteria for project postings.

## Out of Scope
- Advanced analytics for matching algorithms.
- Comprehensive user dashboard and reporting tools.
- Extensive legal frameworks or employment contracts for various countries beyond foundational documentations.
- Full-scale marketing efforts beyond initial outreach.

## Business Model Hypothesis
SkillBridge may charge a small fee to organizations for posting projects or successfully matching with a candidate while remaining free for graduates. This fee structure could be based on project completion or a subscription model for companies with ongoing project needs.

## Critical Assumptions
- Graduates are motivated to engage in short-term projects for experience.
- Companies see value in using the platform for project-based work versus traditional hiring.
- The matching process will effectively pair suitable candidates with relevant projects.
- Feedback and reputation systems will establish necessary trust between users.

## How to Test Quickly
- Conduct surveys with recent graduates and small businesses to validate interest.
- Create landing pages to gauge interest and collect emails before MVP development.
- Interview potential users to gather insights on desired features and functionalities.
- Define success criteria for user feedback loops to ensure they provide benefits to all parties involved.

## Acceptance Criteria
- At least 50 projects posted within the first 3 months.
- A minimum of 100 registered graduates within the first month post-launch.
- At least 75% of users indicating satisfaction with their project experience through feedback surveys.
- Successful legal compliance documentation is established prior to the launch.

## Risks and Failure Modes
- Difficulty attracting enough companies and projects to establish a user base.
- Risk of low-quality projects leading to poor user experience.
- Legal issues or misunderstandings regarding employment qualifications and labor laws.
- The platform may fail to effectively match candidates with appropriate projects.

## Product Readiness
Status: LIMITED

Blocking Gaps:
- Uncertainty about specific legal frameworks applicable to project-based work.
- Limited understanding of effective user acquisition strategies.

Required Improvements:
- [legal_navigability] Establish foundational legal frameworks to ensure compliance and trust regarding project-based work, so, the manual review process includes explicit criteria for legitimacy.
- [user_acquisition] Develop specific outreach strategies for attracting both recent graduates and organizations.

## Recommendation
Proceed to build a limited MVP targeting recent graduates with a simple user interface for profiles, project postings, and basic matching capabilities. Simultaneously conduct user research and develop required legal frameworks during project review to maintain quality and compliance.
