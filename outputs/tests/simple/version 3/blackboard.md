# Dog Dating App Proposal

## Product Problem
Current pet dating solutions lack an engaging, humorous approach that resonates with dog owners who value personality and fun in their pets' social interactions. The existing platforms tend to be generic and fail to foster genuine connections based on shared quirks and personality traits of dogs.

## Initial Wedge
Create a "Dog Dating" app that features quirky profiles for dogs, allowing pet owners to connect based on fun and humorous traits. This app will leverage humor and irony to differentiate itself from more traditional pet social platforms.

## First Target User
Dog owners aged 25-40 who enjoy a light-hearted approach to pet companionship, are active on social media, and appreciate humor in pet-related experiences.

## Existing Alternatives And Switching Trigger
Current alternatives include generic pet adoption sites and social media groups. Users may switch to the dog dating app when it provides a novel and entertaining way to connect with other dog owners that traditional platforms do not offer.

## Core MVP Workflow
1. User registration and dog profile creation.
2. Fun and quirky dog personality traits input (with humor prompts).
3. Simple matching based on dogs’ profiles where both users can view and interact.
4. In-app messaging to facilitate connections.

## In Scope
- User registration.
- Dog profile creation with humor prompts.
- Matching algorithm based on personality traits.
- In-app messaging functionality.

## Out of Scope
- Advanced features like dog events or community-building functions.
- Premium subscription models for advanced matching or features.
- Integration with third-party platforms (social media).

## Business Model Hypothesis
The app will be free to download with potential monetization through advertising partnerships with pet-related businesses and brands that align with the app’s humor theme.

## Critical Assumptions
1. Pet owners value humor and irony in social interactions and are willing to engage with a dedicated app for this purpose.
2. Users can be encouraged to invest time in creating fun profiles based on prompts.

## How To Test Quickly
- Conduct surveys with target users to assess interest in humor-driven pet dating.
- Test the initial concept with a simple landing page showcasing potential app features to gauge user interest and collect emails.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Successful registration and profile setup for at least 100 users within the first month of launch.
- At least 50 matches made in the first month.
- User feedback indicating enjoyment of the humor and overall experience.

## Risks And Failure Modes
- Users may not adopt a humorous approach as expected, leading to disengagement.
- Competition from existing pet dating solutions might overwhelm a niche offering.
- Technical issues may arise in the matching algorithm that hinders user experience.
